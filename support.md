# PlainCam Support

If you need help with PlainCam, open an issue on the PlainCam public support repository:

[https://github.com/iHandle/plaincam-site/issues](https://github.com/iHandle/plaincam-site/issues)

Please include:

- iPhone model.
- iOS version.
- PlainCam version.
- What you expected to happen.
- What actually happened.

# PlainCam 支持

如果你需要 PlainCam 支持，请在 PlainCam 公开支持仓库提交 issue：

[https://github.com/iHandle/plaincam-site/issues](https://github.com/iHandle/plaincam-site/issues)

请尽量包含：

- iPhone 型号。
- iOS 版本。
- PlainCam 版本。
- 你预期发生什么。
- 实际发生了什么。
