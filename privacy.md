# PlainCam Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-17

PlainCam is a local camera app for iPhone. It is designed to capture photos and save the selected output files to the user's Photos library.

## Data Collection

PlainCam does not require an account. PlainCam does not upload photos. PlainCam does not use advertising tracking, third-party analytics SDKs, or third-party crash reporting SDKs.

## Camera Access

PlainCam uses camera permission for live preview and photo capture.

## Photos Access

PlainCam uses photo library add permission to save captured photos to the system Photos library.

PlainCam uses photo library read permission to show photos saved by PlainCam in the in-app gallery. PlainCam keeps a local list of PhotoKit `PHAsset.localIdentifier` values so it can recognize photos that were saved by PlainCam.

## Data Sharing

PlainCam does not sell, share, or transfer user photos or photo metadata to third parties.

## Contact

For support, visit [PlainCam Support](https://ihandle.github.io/plaincam-site/support/).

# PlainCam 隐私政策

最后更新：2026-05-17

PlainCam 是一款本地 iPhone 相机 App，用于拍照，并将用户选择的输出文件保存到系统照片图库。

## 数据收集

PlainCam 不要求账号，不上传照片，不使用广告追踪，不接入第三方 analytics SDK，也不接入第三方 crash SDK。

## 相机权限

PlainCam 使用相机权限进行实时取景和拍照。

## 照片权限

PlainCam 使用照片添加权限，将拍摄的照片保存到系统照片图库。

PlainCam 使用照片读取权限，在 App 内展示由 PlainCam 保存过的照片。PlainCam 会在本机保存 PhotoKit 的 `PHAsset.localIdentifier` 列表，用于识别哪些照片由 PlainCam 保存。

## 数据共享

PlainCam 不会出售、共享或传输用户照片及照片 metadata 给第三方。

## 联系方式

如需支持，请访问 [PlainCam Support](https://ihandle.github.io/plaincam-site/support/)。
